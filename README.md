## Biography

I am an undergraduate student of the computer sciences, and in my free time I like to carry out machine learning, deep learning, and confidence machine learning studies on medical datasets. In 2020 this little side passion of mine became part of my career as a biomedical ML research scientist when I performed a cardiovascular disease clustering study with an accompanying paper written in LaTeX. Since then I have won the Santander Universities Global Challenges COVID-19 Research Scholarship involving the diagnosis of COVID-19 using smartphone recorded coughs, using a novel algorithm to segment the audio samples and perform Conformal Prediction on the dataset. My study is currently being published and will be available in the coming months. I will also be attending the conference CoPA 2022: The 11th Symposium on Conformal and Probabilistic Prediction with Applications.

I am looking to perform more machine learning and deep learning studies focusing on the diagnosis and prognosis of disease, specifically pulmonary diseases such as lung cancers and pneumonia, but I have also taken an interest in cardiovascular diseases. I specialise mostly in the use of numerical and audio datasets, but lately I have taken an avid interest in radiology and image datasets. I also wouldn't be opposed to working on some bioinformatics datasets. I am particularly interested in classification and regression using neural networks - Artificial Neural Networks (ANN), Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) and Recurrent Neural Networks (RNN) - as well as Multi-Layer Perceptrons (MLP), K-Nearest Neighbours (KNN), Decision Trees (DT), and Support Vector Machines (SVM). I'd like to investigate Generative Adversarial Networks (GAN) as well. I'm also interested in clustering studies using K-Means and K-Modes, and lastly ensemble classification and regression using Random Forests (RF), bagging, stacking, and voting models.

I am also looking to perform more confidence machine learning studies for both classification and regression, using the Conformal Prediction framework - specifically Inductive Conformal Prediction (ICP), Mondrian Inductive Conformal Prediction (MICP), and Mondrian Cross-Conformal Prediction (MCCP). In addition, I am interested in researching the class imbalance problem, evaluation metrics for imbalanced data, undersampling and oversampling techniques (and SMOTE or Synthetic Minority Oversampling Technique), cost-sensitive learning, and classification and regression boosting. Lastly, I would like to work on a few multiclass and one-class classification studies, as both are useful for rare disease diagnosis, such as rare genetic diseases.
